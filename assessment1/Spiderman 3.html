Synopsis

Ah, the long awaited Spiderman 3. Here Peter Parker (Tom Holland) tries to erase the world's memory of him being Spiderman with the help of Dr Strange (Benedict Cumberbatch) however, things don't quite work out and he ends up ripping a hole in space-time. This event creates an opening for individuals to come through from the multiverse. This leads to most of a group of past Spiderman villains, the Sinister 6, to come through and wreak havoc on New York City and the rest of the world. The world's only hope is Spiderman and his friends, can they all make it out alive?
